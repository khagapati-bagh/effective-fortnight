CODEXTER

      CODEXTER: Do you think you can handle codes with dexterity? If YES, this is your chance to take on some coding problems and solve it to compete against other participants with similar skills and interest. This is a coding event. Two participants from each college can participate in CODEXTER. It will have 2 rounds.

Rules:

  • 1. Only 2 participants can participate from each team.
  • 2. It will have 2 rounds. Only 5 teams will qualify for final round.
  • 3. First round contains 30 multiple choice questions from C/C++ and simple coding problems, for this round you will have only 45 minutes.
  • 4. Final round, this is a coding round and will be based on “PIPE” structure of coding, for this round you will have only 60 minutes.

DATAMAZE

       How does it feel to find your way out of a maze full of data? This is a data centric event in which two participants will be participating from each college. DATAMAZE will have two rounds.

Rules:

  • 1. Only 2 participants can participate from each team.
  • 2. It will have 2 rounds. Only 5 teams will qualify for the final round of DATAMAZE.
  • 3. First round contain MCQ’s from SQL&PL/SQL. Time allotted for this round will be 30 minutes.
  • 4. In Final round the participants will be given multiple tables. The participants will be asked to fetch some required data from the provided tables. Time allotted for this round will be 60 minutes.
